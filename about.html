About Us

Welcome to Ofuka
International Inc.
- Your Global
Automotive Partner

Established in 1993, Ofuka International Inc.
has been a trusted name in the automotive
industry for over three decades. With an
unwavering commitment to premium service
quality and an unparalleled wealth of
experience, we have solidified our position as a leading source for high-quality vehicles directly from
Japan’s expansive car auction market.

Our Mission

At Ofuka International Inc., our mission is to
bridge distances, bringing the world of
Japanese
automobiles right to your doorstep,
no matter where you are located. We
understand the challenges of finding reliable
and competitively priced vehicles, and we are dedicated to exceeding your expectations at every step of the process.

Our Expertise

Navigating the dynamic automotive landscape requires exceptional industry knowledge and efficiency.
Our team’s expertise extends from meticulous pre-bidding research to seamless vehicle delivery,
ensuring a safe and trouble-free transition from Japan to your destination.
We take pride in keeping you informed and engaged throughout the entire journey, no matter the distance.

Global Reach

While our roots are in Japan, our services know no bounds. We have successfully exported
Japanese
vehicles to a diverse range of
destinations including Hong Kong, Macau,
Singapore, Malaysia,
Indonesia, Thailand,
Dubai, Australia, and the United Kingdom. Our reach is continually expanding, as we embrace new opportunities and markets across the
globe.

Embracing the Future

As we look ahead, our vision extends beyond
Japan’s borders. We are committed to
evolving further,
ensuring that we can deliver
vehicles from around the world to your
doorstep. Our journey is one of
perpetual
innovation, allowing us to seamlessly connect
you with vehicles from every corner of the
globe.
